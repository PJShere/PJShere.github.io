Nestled in the Western Ghats in the Southern Indian state of TamilNadu, KodaiKanal resembles more of an Old English town than anything else.

The air is chilly and the fog seems to be always there adding to the mystical aura. Enchanting Pine Tree forests and little stone cottages embellish the landscape with an old world charm.
